About Me

My name is Ethan Perez and I am a third year Business Honors and Management Information Systems major in the McCombs School of Business at UT Austin. Along with working on cool technology projects, I'm an avid sports enthusiast and geek (daily fantasy, anyone?). I gain a great joy from building systems that solve inefficiencies, most likely because I never stopped loving LEGOs. I've self-taught myself most of the web and native software engineering skills that I have, and I'm constantly soaking up more. In the Spring of 2014, I worked at Texas 4000 as the IT Intern, and I spent my Fall working as a Technology Assistant for the UT Athletics Department.
